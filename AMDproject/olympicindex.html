2018 PyeongChang Winter Olympics

The XXIII Olympic Winter Games will be held for 17 days from February 9th to February 25th in PyeongChang, Gangwon Province, the Republic of Korea. PyeongChang was selected as the host city of the 2018 Olympic Winter Games after receiving a majority vote at the 123rd IOC Session held on July 6, 2011 after three consecutive bids.
